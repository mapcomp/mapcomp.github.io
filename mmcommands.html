Command Description
mc help Gives you the link to this page and soon an embed too.
mc suggest/complain/ask/add Ask the creator anything anonymously from adding a new command to adding your competition.
mc invite Gives you the bot's invite link.
mc serverinfo Gives you information about the server the command is ran in.
mc userinfo Gives you information about the user whose ID is posted or is mentioned.
mc server/blog/updates Gives you the link to the support and update server.
mc welcome Tells you how to activate custom welcome messages and auto-role.
mc timer Self-explanatory, ay?
mc clear/purge Purges a specific amount of messages. (Manage Messages required.)
mc kick Kicks a specific user. (Kick Members required.)
mc ban Bans a specific user. (Ban Members required.)
mc mute Mutes a specific user. (Kick Members required.)
mc unmute Unmutes a specific user. (Kick Members required.)
mc unban Unbans a specific user. (Ban Members required.)
